Community

What is the only thing better than reading a good book?

Reading good books together. We thrive on a like minded community of readers here at Book Haven, and we encourage all our customers to come spend time together. We do monthly voting to see what book we will add to the book club list next and bring our community together to have coffee and book discussion on Thursday evening after closing time from 6 pm - 9 pm. We love to have new members come and join our community. Come on and lets all get lost in the stories together
